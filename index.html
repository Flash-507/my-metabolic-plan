South Indian Diet Protocol

Strategic food choices to manage hypertension (low sodium) and fatty liver/prediabetes (low glycemic index).

✅ Prioritize

  • ✔ Carbs: Ragi dosa, jowar roti, pesarattu, oats upma.
  • ✔ Curries: Sambar, kootu, poriyal (minimal oil), rasam.
  • ✔ Veg: Bitter gourd, drumstick, ivy gourd, okra, cluster beans.
  • ✔ Fruits: Guava, jamun, amla, pomegranate, papaya, apple.
  • ✔ Proteins: Grilled fish, boiled chicken (no skin), moong dal.
  • ✔ Daily: Fenugreek seeds (soaked), curry leaves.

❌ Limit or Avoid

  • ✖ Carbs: White rice in large portions, rava upma, maida parotta.
  • ✖ Fried: Deep-fried items: bonda, vada, bajji, murukku.
  • ✖ BP Risk: Pickles, papads (high salt raises BP dangerously).
  • ✖ High Sugar: Mango, grapes, sapota, jackfruit.
  • ✖ Allergy/Fat: Mutton, prawns, crabs (allergy confirmed).
  • ✖ Drinks: Sweetened filter coffee/tea in excess.
